## Start With The Useful Context

I read specific messages carefully. A short, direct note is enough if it explains what exists today, what is blocked, and what kind of help would make the next step clearer.

Good fits usually involve:

- product engineering and technical product work
- Nuxt, Vue, TypeScript, GraphQL, and Cloudflare systems
- dashboards, internal tools, and operational workflows
- AI-assisted software workflows with practical constraints
- technical writing, documentation, and open-source collaboration

If the topic is a project, include the product stage, expected role, timeline, and any existing links I should review.
