Safarnak is an open-source AI travel workspace: a place to shape trips, compare options, preserve context, and turn planning into something closer to a reusable project.

The long-term idea is a forkable trip planner. A trip should be easy to copy, adapt, discuss, and improve. The product space includes itineraries, maps, constraints, budgets, local notes, and AI-assisted research, but the project should stay grounded in practical travel decisions rather than generic chat.

Current notes for the project:

- Keep the workspace inspectable and editable.
- Treat AI output as draft material, not hidden truth.
- Make planning artifacts easy to reuse.
- Build for web and mobile without making the core workflow heavy.

This page will collect public design notes, implementation decisions, and release milestones as the project develops.
