Mohetios is a builder persona.

The name starts with `Mohet`, a personal and colloquial form connected to "I am". The suffix `-ios` gives it the feeling of an old technical name, half imagined and half inherited from Greek and Roman naming patterns.

That mix matters. The site is personal, but it is also a tool. It gives a stable name to a way of working: design in public, build in small proofs, write down the tradeoffs, and let unfinished ideas remain visible long enough to become useful.

Mohetios is not a company and not a mask. It is a handle for a workshop.
