Mohetios is the public workbench for things I am building, learning, and trying to understand.

This site is not meant to be a polished announcement channel. It is a place for durable notes: decisions that survived implementation, mistakes that taught something useful, project logs that make future maintenance easier, and longer essays about software, product work, and computational ideas.

The shape is intentionally simple. Blog posts hold longer arguments. Lab notes hold fragments and exploratory work. Project pages collect the open-source systems and experiments that need a stable home.

The goal is to keep a record that is technical without becoming sterile, personal without becoming performative, and open enough that someone else can fork an idea and continue the thread.

For now, Mohetios.dev begins with static Markdown, a small Nuxt app, and enough structure to make writing easy.
